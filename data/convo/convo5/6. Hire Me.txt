
A: I need a job.
B: What was your last job?

B: What happened?

B: What else can you do?
A: I'm a handyman.

A: Of course.
B: Then I have a job for you in my kitchen.
A: It will cost you only $20 plus parts.
B: Okay. That sounds like a fair price.
