Expressing Disapointment in English
Here are some useful phrases and expressions you can use to show your disappointment:

http://basicenglishspeaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/audio/ce/E-46.mp3
 
 That’s too bad.
 How disappointing!
 That’s so disappointing!
 Oh no, what a shame!
 It’s a great shame.
 It’s a little bit disappointing, isn’t it!
 It didn’t live up to my expectations.
 What a pity!
 What a letdown!
 What a bummer!
 That’s a nuisance!
 I thought you could do better.
 I’ve never been so disappointed in my life.
 It wasn’t as good as I thought it would be.
