Making Friends
Below are some common sentences and expressions you can use when you meet new people and you want to make friends with them.

http://basicenglishspeaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/audio/ce/E-32.mp3
 
 It’s nice to meet you.
 What’s your name?
 I’m John.
 I’m sorry, I didn’t catch your name.
 Where are you from?
 I’m from the U.S.
 What brings you here?
 I’m on holiday.
 What’s your phone number?
 Could I take your phone number?
 May I have your address?
 Are you on Facebook?
 Who do you live with?
 I live on my own.
 I live with my friends.
 How old are you?
 I’m 30.
 When’s your birthday?
 It’s 16th June.
