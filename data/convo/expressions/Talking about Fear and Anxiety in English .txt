Talking about Fear and Anxiety in English
Here are some useful phrases and expressions you can you when talking about your fear and anxiety.

http://basicenglishspeaking.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/audio/ce/E-25.mp3
 
 Are you afraid of the dark?
 I’m afraid of being alone at night.
 I’m scared of snakes.
 I’m frightened of spiders.
 I am terrified.
 I get scared very easily.
 I was terrified when I heard the noise outside.
 I’m worried about her health.
Useful Responses:
 Don’t be afraid.
 It’s not a big deal.
 It is nothing.
 There is nothing to be afraid of.
 Take is easy.
 Calm down.
